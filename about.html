Unsurpassed Quality Control

At Merchia Global, we recognize that maintaining strict quality control is crucial for Indian makhana exporters to gain the confidence of discriminating global wholesale clients. For the purpose of guaranteeing that only the best makhana kernels are exported from our facilities in Bihar to the global market, our company has set up sophisticated sorting and grading infrastructure. To grade harvested fox nuts according to size, shape, color, and texture, we have made an investment in specialist equipment. We categorize top export-grade, Grade A fox nuts that adhere to exacting quality standards using specialized mechanical sieves and optical sorting equipment. Furthermore, we examine in the lab representative samples from each export batch at random to assess important characteristics that foreign importers require, including moisture content, sugar content, freshness, flavor, consistency, and crunchiness.

The impartial Quality Evaluation Laboratory of the Spices Board of India conducts quality standards tests on all spices shipped by Merchia Global. This laboratory is accredited by the National Accreditation Board for Testing & Calibration Laboratories (NABL) and has been certified by the British Standards Institution, U.K., since 1999 for the ISO 9001:2008 Quality Management System and ISO 14001:2004 Environmental Management System. For instance, among other checks, we look for ethylene oxide in saffron, tiny cardamom, and cinnamon, as well as aflatoxin in turmeric and Sudan dye LCMS. In conclusion, you can be sure that Merchia Global will provide you with only the best products, which honour the rich history of Indian spices, when you choose them for your needs.
